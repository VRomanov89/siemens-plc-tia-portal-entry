# Section 2 — Setting Up TIA Portal: Introduction

**Estimated segment duration:** 3–5 minutes
**Type:** Section orientation (structural — not counted in lesson totals)

---

## What You'll Learn in This Section

This section is about getting TIA Portal installed and functional without hitting the obstacles that knock most beginners off course before they've written a single rung of logic.

In this section you'll cover two things:

1. **Navigating the Siemens download ecosystem** — which version of TIA Portal to install, what the 21-day trial actually includes, how to handle the PLCSIM export control registration (a step almost no course mentions), and what your PC actually needs to run TIA Portal reliably.

2. **Installing TIA Portal and getting oriented in the interface** — what components to install and what to skip, and a guided tour of every major area of the TIA Portal workspace so nothing feels foreign when you start building your first project.

This section is short, but it earns its place. The number one reason beginners stall before writing any code is an installation or setup problem that wasn't covered in the course they were following. This section covers all of them.

---

## What You'll Need

- A PC meeting minimum requirements (covered in Lesson 2.1)
- A Siemens ID — free to create at id.siemens.com
- An active internet connection for downloading software and submitting export control registration
